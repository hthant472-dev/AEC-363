📜 Historical Evolution of Agricultural Technology

(ခေတ်အလိုက် စိုက်ပျိုးရေးတော်လှန်ရေး အချက်အလက်များကို သိရှိလိုပါက သက်ဆိုင်ရာအဝိုင်းလေးများကို နှိပ်ကြည့်ပါ)

Second Agricultural Revolution(17th -19th Century)

Crop rotation systems, selective breeding, and mechanization with steam-powered equipment.

Green Revolution(1960s-1970s)

HYV seeds, synthetic fertilizers, and pesticides. Development of dwarf wheat varieties.

Gene Revolution(1990s-2000s)

Introduction of GMOs with traits like herbicide tolerance and insect resistance in soybeans, corn, and cotton.

Digital Revolution(2010s-Present)

Integration of IT, AI, robotics, and biotechnology creating precision agriculture systems.

Integrated Pest Management System Strategies

Chemical Methods in IPM Strategy

When chemical controls are necessary, IPM emphasizes:

🎯
Economic threshold-based application Treating only when pest populations exceed damaging levels.
ကောင်
Selective products Choosing pesticides with lower impact on natural enemies.
📍
Targeted application Using precision technology to apply only where needed.
🌿
Resistance management Rotating modes of action and using mixtures.
⏰
Timing optimization Applying when pests are most vulnerable and beneficials are least active.
